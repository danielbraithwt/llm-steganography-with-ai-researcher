## Key Synthesis

### V ≥ K Decodability Explained
The "contradiction" between Phase 1 (K > V causality) and Phase 2 (V ≥ K decodability)
is resolved: K carries the routing program (WHERE), V carries the content data (WHAT).
Destroying routing (K) is catastrophic; probing content (V) yields the answer.
Zhang et al. (2026) and Anthropic's QK-OV coupling work independently confirm this.

### Experiment C (WRRA) is Well-Motivated
Sun et al. (EMNLP 2025) achieve >90% accuracy probing residual stream for correct answers
at error positions. Our unique contribution will be K/V decomposition at these positions.
Expected: V-probe > K-probe for correct value (V=content), with effect in deep layers.

### Our Unique Contribution Remains Clear
All probing papers probe the RESIDUAL STREAM. None decompose into K vs V. Our K/V
decomposition reveals the routing-content duality — this is our distinctive methodology.

### Steganographic Implications Are Growing
4 papers in 2025-2026 address steganographic computation in LLMs. Our hidden channel
finding has safety implications: the KV cache routing program is architecturally hidden
from text-level monitoring. This is more fundamental than text-level steganography.
